Press-On Nails

Get salon-quality nails without the hassle! We only use Apres Gel-X full-cover tips, which give you that professional strength and flawless look - without the bulky feel. Skip the long salon visits and just press them on; you’ll be ready to go in minutes. Plus, they’re reusable - just pop them off, file off the glue, and rock your set again up to three times. And here’s the best part: Since the gel is already cured, there’s no risk of gel allergies - just beautiful, worry-free nails every time!

Press-On Sizing Kit

Finding your perfect nail fit has never been easier! With a press-on nail sizing kit, you can quickly match each finger to its ideal size - no guessing, no stress. The kit lets you test different sizes in your preferred shape so your press-ons feel comfortable and look flawlessly natural. And with Apres Gel-X’s half-sizes, you get an even more precise fit for every nail, ensuring a smooth, seamless look from thumb to pinky. It’s the simple, foolproof way to get salon-perfect nails that feel custom-made just for you! Please use this easy Press-On Guide to determine what shapes we have available.

Nail Art Vinyls

More into DIY? Nail art vinyls make it easy to unleash your creativity and design like a pro! Use them as fun decorations on their own or as handy stencils to craft custom patterns, crisp lines, and eye-catching details. Whether you’re going for chic geometric shapes, playful patterns, or intricate accents, vinyls help you get a flawless finish without the guesswork. Just stick, paint, and peel for instant nail art magic - perfect for beginners and pros alike!

Waterslide Decals

Waterslide decals make creating stunning nail art effortless and fun! Simply soak, slide, and apply - no steady hand or advanced painting skills required. These ultra-thin designs lay smoothly on the nail, giving a professional, hand-painted look in minutes. Best of all, they can be custom designed to match your unique style - whether you want elegant florals, bold graphics, or personalized art that’s totally your own. With waterslide decals, you get gorgeous, one-of-a-kind nails with minimal effort and maximum creativity!

Jewelry Made with Nail Polish

Each piece of my nail polish jewelry is a tiny work of art - handcrafted with layers of vibrant, shimmering polish to create depth, color, and light you won’t find anywhere else. Because I use real nail polish - including rare and discontinued shades - every pendant, ring, and pair of earrings is truly one of a kind. The result is jewelry that captures the sparkle and personality of nail art in a wearable, lasting form. Whether you’re drawn to bold statement pieces or soft, ethereal hues, you’ll find something beautifully unique - just like you.

About

Hello! My name is Blu and I started doing nail art as a hobby in 2011. If you check my portfolio, you will see my evolution over the years. I started DIY nails at home with techniques like sponging and taping, then I discovered stamping and was completely obsessed. I currently have a polish collection of 5000-ish bottles, and around 1000 unique stamping plates. Of course, I have stones, studs, piercing tools, foils, powders, and just about every kind of embellishment that has come out over the years. I love to experiment and try new techniques. If you would like to collab on a custom set of press-ons, get in touch using the contact form!
