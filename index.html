About Me!

Hello! My name is Blu and I started doing nail art as a hobby in 2011. If you check my portfolio, you will see my evolution over the years. I started DIY nails at home with techniques like sponging and taping, then I discovered stamping and was completely obsessed. I currently have a polish collection of 5000-ish bottles, and around 1000 unique stamping plates. Of course I have stones, studs, piercing tools, foils, powders, and just about every kind of embellishment that has come out over the years. I love to experiment and try new techniques. If you would like to collab on a custom set of press-ons, get in touch using the contact form!
